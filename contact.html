Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about working with JW Partners.

How long does a typical project take?
Project timelines vary based on scope and complexity. A brand strategy project typically takes 4-8 weeks, while a comprehensive rebrand can take 3-6 months. We'll provide a detailed timeline during our initial consultation.
What is your pricing structure?
We offer both project-based pricing and monthly retainers. Project prices range from $2,500 for smaller initiatives to $50,000+ for comprehensive rebrands. Monthly retainers start at $2,500 for basic services.
Do you work with international clients?
Yes, we work with clients worldwide. We have experience helping brands expand into new markets and can accommodate different time zones. Our team includes specialists with international market expertise.
What industries do you specialize in?
We have extensive experience across multiple sectors including luxury hospitality, fashion, technology, healthcare, and consumer goods. Our strategic approach is adaptable to virtually any industry.
How do we get started?
Begin by scheduling a free consultation through our online form or by contacting us directly. We'll discuss your goals, challenges, and determine if we're a good fit for your needs.
What makes JW Partners different?
Our unique 4Ps framework (Place, Power, Path, Position) combined with our multi-disciplinary approach sets us apart. We're not just executors—we're strategic partners invested in your long-term success.
